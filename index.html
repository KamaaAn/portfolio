My name is Kama

I would like to tell you briefly about me and my work experience. Originally from Warsaw, Poland, but now residing in Toronto, I'm looking to grow personally and professionally in Canada.

I have over 8 years of experience in the media, agency, and client side of digital marketing projects. My favourite part has always been in creating digital solutions and design: websites, shops, applications, landing pages, mailings. It’s always been about how to build a perfect tool to meet the client’s needs while creating something beautiful and having fun.

I decided to develop the right skills. I started with WordPress websites for small companies as a freelancer and now have built them for over a year. It wasn’t enough, so that's why I just finished Frontend developer bootcamp. It gave me a lot of fresh knowledge, but also even more enthusiasm for Frontend jobs!

I want to be involved in projects that I can be proud of, join an amazing team, and start a real Frontend development career. If you're interested in helping me make this happen, let's get in touch.

In private, I’m a big fan of sci-fi movies and fantasy books (mostly Polish authors), yoga freak and a cat lover. I’ve travelled across 4 continents, visited 25 countries and I’m still looking for more!
